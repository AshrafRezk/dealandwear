# Affiliate Program Research Guide

## Overview
This document provides a guide for researching and applying to affiliate programs for Egyptian e-commerce stores.

## Target Stores

### Priority 1: Major E-commerce Platforms

#### 1. Noon Egypt
- **Website**: https://www.noon.com
- **Affiliate Program**: Noon Affiliate Program
- **Contact**: Look for "Become a Partner" or "Affiliate Program" in footer
- **Requirements**: 
  - Website/blog with traffic
  - Social media presence
  - Business registration (may be required)
- **Commission**: Typically 2-5% of sales
- **API Access**: May provide product feeds or API after approval
- **Application Process**:
  1. Visit noon.com
  2. Scroll to footer, look for "Partners" or "Affiliate"
  3. Fill out application form
  4. Wait for approval (1-2 weeks)

#### 2. Namshi
- **Website**: https://www.namshi.com
- **Affiliate Program**: Namshi Affiliate Program
- **Contact**: partners@namshi.com or affiliate program page
- **Requirements**: Similar to Noon
- **Commission**: Varies by category
- **Notes**: Part of Emaar group, may have stricter requirements

#### 3. Shein
- **Website**: https://eg.shein.com
- **Affiliate Program**: Shein Affiliate Program
- **Contact**: Look for "Influencer Program" or "Affiliate" in footer
- **Requirements**: 
  - Social media following (Instagram, TikTok, etc.)
  - Content creation ability
- **Commission**: Typically 10-20% (higher than others)
- **API Access**: May provide product feeds
- **Application**: Usually through influencer portal

### Priority 2: Fashion Brands

#### 4. H&M Egypt
- **Website**: https://www2.hm.com/en_eg
- **Affiliate Program**: H&M Affiliate Program (if available in Egypt)
- **Contact**: Check global affiliate program, may not be available in Egypt
- **Notes**: May need to contact regional office

#### 5. Zara Egypt
- **Website**: https://www.zara.com/eg
- **Affiliate Program**: Usually not available (brand policy)
- **Alternative**: Focus on product discovery, link to official site
- **Notes**: Zara typically doesn't have affiliate programs

#### 6. Max Fashion
- **Website**: https://www.maxfashion.com
- **Affiliate Program**: Check for partner program
- **Contact**: Contact through website
- **Notes**: May have regional affiliate programs

### Priority 3: Local Egyptian Stores

#### 7. Local Fashion Brands
- Research Egyptian fashion brands
- Contact directly via email/phone
- Offer value proposition: exposure, traffic, sales
- May be more flexible with terms

## Application Template

### Email Template for Affiliate Program Application

**Subject**: Affiliate Program Application - Deal & Wear Fashion Platform

**Body**:
```
Dear [Store Name] Team,

I am reaching out to inquire about your affiliate/partner program for Deal & Wear, a fashion discovery and product search platform focused on the Egyptian market.

About Deal & Wear:
- Fashion product search and discovery platform
- AI-powered style recommendations
- Focus on Egyptian e-commerce stores
- Growing user base interested in fashion and shopping

What We Offer:
- Quality traffic from fashion-interested users
- Product discovery and comparison features
- Direct links to your products
- Potential for increased sales and brand exposure

We would like to:
1. Join your affiliate program
2. Access product feeds/API (if available)
3. Display your products on our platform
4. Drive qualified traffic to your store

Please let me know:
- Application process
- Commission structure
- Available product data/API access
- Any requirements or documentation needed

Thank you for your consideration. I look forward to partnering with [Store Name].

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
Deal & Wear
[Contact Information]
```

## Research Checklist

- [ ] Visit each store's website
- [ ] Check footer for "Affiliate", "Partner", "Become a Partner" links
- [ ] Search for "[Store Name] affiliate program"
- [ ] Check if they have API documentation
- [ ] Look for product feed availability
- [ ] Note commission rates
- [ ] Document application requirements
- [ ] Track application status
- [ ] Follow up after 1-2 weeks if no response

## Alternative Approaches

### 1. Google Shopping API
- **Free**: Yes
- **Requirements**: Google account, API key
- **Coverage**: Aggregates products from multiple stores
- **Setup**: 1-2 days
- **Status**: Can implement immediately

### 2. Product Feed Aggregators
- Some services aggregate product feeds
- May require subscription
- Research: "Egypt product feed API"

### 3. Direct Partnerships
- Contact stores directly
- Offer value beyond just affiliate links
- Propose data sharing or co-marketing

## Tracking Progress

Create a spreadsheet to track:
- Store name
- Contact method
- Application date
- Status (pending, approved, rejected)
- Commission rate
- API access (yes/no)
- Notes
